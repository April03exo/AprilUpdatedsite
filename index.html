ABOUT

A little peek into the system maker

Hi! I'm April Joy V. Rapanut. I may be 20 but still act like a child sometimes. You can find me in Nagtupacan, Sta. Maria Ilocos Sur or at our church, which has always been my second home.

My Hobbies Reading, watching anime, sketching, writing, and capturing beautiful photos. These simple passions help me escape reality sometimes.

Outside the church, you'll find me diving into novels, exploring sceneries with my camera, and letting my imagination flow through sketching. I also enjoy writing random thoughts and reflections sometimes personal, sometimes just dreamy ideas about life.

I value my parents, brother, and closest friends inspire me to keep going, even during the hardest times. I believe life is better when shared with the people you love.

As a student of Bachelor of Science in Information Systems, I enjoy reading books and imagining how innovations can shape the future.

I dream of traveling the world, experiencing different cultures, capturing breathtaking sunsets, and writing my journey in my diary. I also hope to build a career in technology where I can create something useful, but at the same time live a life filled with faith and creativity.
